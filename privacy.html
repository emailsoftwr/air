Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how this website handles information when you browse, click affiliate links, and interact with analytics or tracking used for performance measurement.

Cookies & Similar Technologies

Cookies may be used for analytics and affiliate attribution. These help understand which pages perform well and which links users click, without revealing sensitive personal identity information.

How We Use Information

Data Sharing

We may share limited data with service providers that help run this website (analytics, hosting, affiliate tracking). Those providers may process data under their own privacy policies and contractual obligations.

Your Choices

Children’s Privacy

This website is not intended for children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy
